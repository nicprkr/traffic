About Us

Traffic is based out of Vancouver, B.C., Canada. Traffic was founded in 2007 after Prime and Rough Riders, the two women's team in Vancouver at the time, merged into one.

Since being formed, Traffic has been chasing success across the world. The team has won Canadian Nationals 4 times and has competed at World Ultimate Club Championships three times. In 2021, we placed 5th at USAU Nationals and 2nd at Ultimate Canada Invitational, securing a bid to the 2022 World Ultimate Club Championships.
